Our Function
Thamer is a dynamic website that helps companies in Saudi Arabia to comply with the Local Content Authority and Saudi Aramco's IKTVA program.

Problem

Companies in Saudi Arabia are required to have a certain percentage of local content and IKTVA in their operations. This can be difficult to achieve, as the market is constantly changing and it can be difficult to find suppliers with high local content and IKTVA scores.

Solution

Thamer provides a one-stop solution for companies to manage their local content and IKTVA compliance. Companies can register on our website and enter their full data, including their local content and IKTVA percentages for the current year and the past five years. This data is then displayed in a table format, making it easy for companies to track their performance and identify areas for improvement.

Benefits

Using Thamer can help companies to save money through winning the government and Aramco contracts, and reduce their risk of non-compliance with Iktva and local content scores.

Target Customer

Thamer is for all companies located in and outside the kingdom of Saudi Arabia that have Iktva and local content scores.
